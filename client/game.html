How to Compete

Step One:
Before a tournament has started, predictions may be made by entering a prediction price and clicking "Predict". Doing so will send your predicted price along with your buyin (the cost to predict) to the tournament contract. Players may make as many predictions as they wish before the tournament starts. Once the tournament starts, no more predictions will be accepted. NOTICE: Predictions should be made to the nearest whole number!


Step Two:
At the conclusion of a tournament, a settlement price will be recorded and a 24 hour resolution period will begin. During the resolution period players with accurate predictions should press "claim winning predictions" in order to claim their most accurate prediction as the winning prediction. The current winning prediction will be on active display so players will know only to claim a winning prediction if their prediction is more accurate!


Step Three:
Once the 24 hour resolution period concludes; Any player who claimed the winning prediction is eligible to withdraw the total contract balance minus a flat 2% fee. If there is more than one winner, then the winnings will be divided equally among them.

Note: A 2% fee is applied to the total prize pool to be collected by Trader Tournaments and the affiliated Tournament creator. We welcome all to create, promote, and profit from their own custom Tournaments here!
