Tournament Lobby

Instructions

Step One: Before the tournament has started, Fill out a predicted price and click "Predict" in order to send a prediction and staked ETH to the Tournament Contract. Players may make as many predictions as they would like.


Step Two: After the tournament has ended, a settlement price will be recorded and players will be able to press "Claim Winning Prediciton" in order to claim their closest prediction as the winner.


Step Three: After the 24 hour resolution period has ended, the player who claimed the prediction closest to the settlement price will be eligible to withdraw their winnings!
