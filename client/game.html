How to Compete

Step One:
Before a tournament has started, players may fill out a predicted price and click "Predict" in order to send a prediction and staked ETH to the Tournament Contract. Players may make as many predictions as they wish.


Step Two:
After the tournament has ended, a settlement price will be recorded and players can press "Claim Winning Prediciton" in order to claim their most accurate prediction as the winner. The current closest claimed prediction will be on display so players only have to claim if their prediciton is more accurate!


Step Three:
After the 24 hour resolution period has concluded, the player who claimed the prediction closest to the settlement price will be eligible to withdraw their winnings!

Note: A 2% fee is applied to the total prize pool to be collected by Trader Tournaments and the affiliated Tournament creator. We welcome all to create, promote, and profit from their own custom Tournaments here!
