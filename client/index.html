Welcome to Trader Tournaments

A decentrilized financial application to create and compete in competitions hosted on the Ethereum Blockchain.

Welcome to the future of decentrilized finance powered by Ethereum



All TraderTournaments are smart contracts living on the Ethereum blockchain. Therefore unlike traditional architectures, customer funds are never in our possession.

/

TraderTournaments attempts to redefine profitable strategies and reinvent how traders compete. Imagine how our strategy changes when we're no longer tasked with optimally trading an open market protocol and instead faced with outperforming one other competitor. We are no longer solely concerned with making profitable trades but rather only focused on outperforming our opponent.

/

A no longer has to compete with every veteran in the open market but can rather compete against whatever player pool in the given TraderTournament.

/

TraderTournaments allows players to compete without ever signing up to create an account. Simply navigate to our Tournament Lobby and join the action! (metamask required)

/

Using our "creator portal" we invite anyone (no coding experience required!) to create their own tournament and collect 50% of the fees generated! Expect more innovative ways to be involved in the future!

Our First Tournament Format

Players compete in our "No Limit Predictions" tournament format by most accurately predicting the price of a given asset at a future date. Players pay a given buyin for each prediction they make that gets added to the total prize pool. Once the price of the asset has settled at the given future date, the player whom predicted the most accurate price is allocated the balance of the contract minus our 2% fee (equally distributed between TraderTournaments and the Tournament Creator).



Find more information on how to compete here!



TraderTournaments is always working on new formats for our players so stay tuned for more!

Fees

At the valid conclusion of a tournament, a 2% fee is reserved to be equally distributed between Trader Tournaments and the Tournament Creator. We are excited to provide anyone the ability to create and promote their own custom tournaments! No coding experience needed, simply use our Tournament Creator portal, and whichever address you use to create a tournament will be credited half the fees at the tournaments conclusion!
